My Top 3 Movies

Watching all of these might cause some level of chemical imbalances and brain anumerysm so try to take it slow.

1. A Silent Voice

A story of a deaf girl and a boy who upon bullying her enters into a cycle of regret and depression leading to suicide attempt feeling like an outcast, this movie delves into mature topics about regret, death, suicide, broken freindships, insecurities and internal manifestation of fears.

2. The Hangover(pt.1 and 2)

4 best friends on a bachelors trip to Sin City, Las Vegas, what could go wrong? Well, a lot. That's what you're about to find out on this hilarious trilogy of movies.
